Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

pig3on, LLC ("pig3on," "we," "us," or "our") operates the pig3on platform, an AI-powered SMS-to-email assistant service for business owners. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website (https://pig3on.io) and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for a pig3on account, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and mobile phone number
  • Your business name and business email address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe; we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Gmail account access (via OAuth) to send and receive emails on your behalf
  • Client inquiry data submitted through your intake forms (names, emails, service details)
  • SMS message content exchanged between you and the pig3on platform
  • AI voice training data you voluntarily provide to customize email drafting style

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the pig3on SMS assistant service
  • Send SMS notifications to your registered mobile number about client inquiries
  • Draft and send email responses on your behalf using AI
  • Process subscription payments
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve and develop our services

3. SMS/Mobile Information

We do not share, sell, or distribute your mobile phone number or any SMS opt-in data to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes at any time. Your phone number is used exclusively to deliver pig3on service notifications related to your account activity and client inquiries.

Message frequency varies based on the volume of client inquiries you receive. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any pig3on message, or by contacting us at support@pig3on.io.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate pig3on:

  • Twilio — SMS message delivery
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Google/Gmail — Email sending and receiving (via your authorized OAuth connection)
  • Supabase — Secure database hosting
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-powered email drafting

These providers access only the data necessary to perform their services and are bound by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, we retain your data for up to 30 days to allow for reactivation, after which it is permanently deleted. You may request immediate deletion by contacting support@pig3on.io.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting support@pig3on.io
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP
  • Revoke Gmail access through your Google account settings
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings

8. Children's Privacy

pig3on is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active users of material changes via email or SMS. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
